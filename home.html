BCF Storytelling

Tired of Losing Stories?

That's why we're here! Our coordinated storytelling allows us to make it easy for you to find all your stories in one place, not losing them through multiple social media sites. It also makes it easier for our coordinators to be abel to reach to the audience that could help them.

How We Spread Stories

Coordinator

Coordinators come from 19 different countries, which they can post stories from their specific country. Their stories have links to allow for donors to directly fund them.

Donors

Donors get a view of all the stories in a order of latest stories, stories can be filtered by country to ensure you can find your specific coordinator. Donors are given the option to donate, allowing them to support stories they like.
